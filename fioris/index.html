Pizza Restaurant in Pittsburgh, PA

Taste the unbeatable flavors of all-natural, hand-tossed pizza made fresh every day at Fiori's Pizzaria. Our locally-owned-and-operated pizza restaurant in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, serves a variety of traditional Italian cuisine from a true Italian family.

Custom Trays

Make your parties for watching the Steelers, Penguins, or high school teams even better with plenty of great food. We use hoagies, wings, or any of our appetizers to create party trays.

Pizza boats are also available!
